Who We Are

We are a Canadian owned private corporation, operating coffee roasting and dry powder blending facilities in London, Ontario with distribution centres in Montreal, Québec and Vancouver, BC.

Our History

Founded in 1980, Heritage Coffee has grown from a regional OCS roaster to one of the Canada’s most important private label roasters. Under the guidance of one of the founders, Stuart Daw, Heritage experienced significant growth, excelling in quality, consistency and innovative packaging options for our partner customers. Now controlled by his son, Kevin Daw – Heritage excels in these values and has expanded its reach to the US market.

Our Products

Coffees and flavoured coffees (packed in various formats), instant cappuccinos, hot chocolates and related products, Allied products (cups, lids, Bigelow Teas, etc.)
