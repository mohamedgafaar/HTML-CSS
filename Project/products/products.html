Black Gold

As black as a long Alaskan winter night – this, the deepest of our roasts, exemplifies the bold characteristics of dark roasting. Dominated by a carbon spiciness, draped with heavy body and brown sugar sweetness. A favorite of dark roast enthusiasts. Brew strong and try pairing with dark chocolate and french vanilla ice cream for a dessert delight

ROAST: dark

Black Wolf Blend

Our award-winning Black Wolf Blend honors the local legend Romeo, a lone yet social black wolf who roamed the Mendenhall Valley for years. A brew for many palates, this blend of coffees from the Americas is perfectly balanced and pleasant any time of day. Packaged in a beautiful valve bag with the story of Romeo on the back. Coffee is available in this 2lb package only.

ROAST: light

Aurora Borealis Blend

Inspired by the Northern Lights that dance in our Alaskan skies, this coffee is distinguished by an exceptionally velvety body. A coffee for many palates, this favored blend exudes notes of almond and dark chocolate.

ROAST: medium-dark
